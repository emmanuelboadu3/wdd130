The Rapids Splash Site Plan

Ghana’s Premier Whitewater Rafting Experience

Site Purpose

To provide a fun, user-friendly website that helps users discover, learn about, and book rafting experiences. We aim to create excitement while ensuring visitors have access to trip info, safety details, and booking features in one place.

Target Audience

  • Who: Adventurous families, nature-loving tourists, first-time rafters, and corporate retreat planners
  • Needs: Safety reassurance, trip previews, group options, guides info
  • Questions:
    • Is this activity safe for children or beginners?
    • What gear do I need to bring?
    • How do I book my group?
    • Are there other nearby activities?
    • Can I get a preview of the river and trip difficulty?
  • Devices: Most users will visit on phones while traveling—so mobile responsiveness is key!
